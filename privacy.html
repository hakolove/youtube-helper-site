Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the "YouTube Helper" desktop application uses and protects your data when you authorize it to access your Google account.

What Data We Access

With your explicit permission, this application may access the following information from your Google account:

  • Your YouTube channel and video metadata (such as titles, descriptions, thumbnails)
  • Your Content ID assets and policy data (if applicable)

How We Use Your Data

All data retrieved via the YouTube API is used exclusively to:

  • Display video metadata in the application UI
  • Allow you to update your own video information
  • Manage Content ID assets and usage policies for your videos

No data is used for analytics, advertising, or sold to third parties.

How We Store Your Data

OAuth tokens and cached information are stored locally on your computer, never transmitted to any server other than Google's APIs. The refresh token is stored securely using the system keyring mechanism.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. No one except you can view your data within the app interface.

Security

This application is built with security and transparency in mind. It does not maintain any backend server that stores user information.

Contact
