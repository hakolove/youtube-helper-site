Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

YT Pilot is a desktop productivity tool designed for managing YouTube content. This policy describes what data we access, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data Accessed

  • Your authorized YouTube channels
  • Video metadata (title, description, thumbnails)
  • Video subtitles (uploaded by you)
  • Content ID assets and policies (if CMS authorized)

2. How We Use the Data

All accessed data is used solely to:

  • Display content inside the YT Pilot app UI
  • Allow you to edit and update your own videos
  • Support automated workflows like bulk updates and claims

3. Data Storage

The application stores your OAuth tokens (such as refresh tokens) securely using your local system’s keychain or credential manager.
No user data is stored or transferred to any external servers. Cache files like reg.dat are also stored locally.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any of your data to third parties.
The application operates entirely between your local machine and Google’s servers.

5. Security

We follow industry best practices to ensure your credentials are not exposed or mishandled. We never read or store your data outside of your machine.

6. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at dev@senyu.tv.

This app is not affiliated with Google or YouTube.
